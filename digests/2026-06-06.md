## Themes

### 1. mx-sdk-rs: CodeMetadata and ESDT validation hardened; GUARDED flag introduced

Two correctness PRs merged on June 5:

- [#2397](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-rs/pull/2397) "CodeMetadata - stricter constructor/convertors" removes the `VMCodeMetadata` alias in favour of `CodeMetadata` throughout scenario and VM host APIs, adds a `CodeMetadataError` type with strict `TryFrom` conversions, and introduces a lenient `from_bytes_or_default` for protocol-mirroring parsing. Critically, it adds a **`GUARDED` code metadata flag** — this is a new protocol-level capability, not a cleanup.
- [#2398](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-rs/pull/2398) "Token id validation fix + refactor + tests" tightens ESDT token identifier validation to enforce a lowercase-hex random suffix, refactors `validate_token_identifier` in `multiversx-chain-core` into smaller helpers with structured error returns, and adds edge-case tests (max ticker length, invalid chars, invalid random-suffix content).

Both are @andrei-marinica. A third PR, [#2399](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-rs/pull/2399) "EsdtLocalRole flag fix, docs, tests", opened June 5 at 15:36 UTC and sits in queue.

Shipping it inside a sprint nominally aimed at v0.67 correctness either (a) widens v0.67's scope beyond what the RC PR implied, or (b) begins v0.68 feature work before v0.67 has tagged. Either way, the "v0.67 is imminent" read from yesterday needs revision.

Three consecutive correctness PRs in <24h after "gates cleared" suggests either a larger pre-release QA sweep is in progress, or the release bar has been raised silently.

At 17 days, this has now persisted across v0.66.1, the v0.67 gate-clear, and two more post-gate merges. This means either the bar for merging is high (review bottleneck) or the PRs are sequentially dependent.

No visible merged PRs emerged June 5, but the cadence matches the pre-release housecleaning pattern observed on May 21 (before v0.66.0) and June 4 (before api-service v1.20.0).
