## Themes

### 1. sc-meta wallet convert: test coverage after Bech32 refactor

[#2379](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-rs/pull/2379) (andrei-marinica, merged May 26) — adds integration-style tests for `sc-meta wallet convert` covering mnemonic→PEM via file IO and stdin/stdout, custom HRP handling, unsupported conversion paths, and expected panics on missing `--infile`. This is the natural follow-up to the Bech32 HRP refactor in [#2377](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-rs/pull/2377): the new `Bech32Hrp` type and wallet convert path now have exercised edge cases. The PR also refactors `SubstitutionsMap::check_subsequence` to use `zip` for match-length computation and documents the "longest prefix" invariant.
