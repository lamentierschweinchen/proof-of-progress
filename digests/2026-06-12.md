### 2. Supernova RC kept current with mainnet fixes

[#7874](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-chain-go/pull/7874) "Merge master supernova june2026" was opened and merged the same day mainnet-config closed. This is deliberate: every v1.11.x stability fix must also land in `rc/supernova` before Supernova activation, otherwise the activation would reintroduce solved bugs. The backport via a named merge branch (`merge-master-supernova-june2026`) instead of a rebase preserves the Supernova branch history clearly.

Simultaneously, [#7879](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-chain-go/pull/7879) synced the RC into `feat/testnet-fixes`, ensuring the testnet-fixes staging branch is also current. Both are AdoAdoAdo's work — he is carrying the branch maintenance responsibility across the three active tracks (mainnet, Supernova RC, testnet-fixes).
