## Themes

### 1. Post-v0.66.0 housekeeping in mx-sdk-rs

[#2372](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-rs/pull/2372) (andrei-marinica, merged May 21) — Windows CI was failing on BLS feature gates after the v0.66.0 release; fix adds an OS-specific feature gate and `$ErrorActionPreference = 'Stop'` to PowerShell CI steps. Signals the team is stabilizing multi-platform CI post-release before cutting a patch version.

### 2. New gas-benchmarking contract: very-large-storage

[#2360](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-rs/pull/2360) (andrei-marinica, merged May 22) — a new benchmark smart contract added to the repo that measures gas consumption when appending to a large `ManagedBuffer` storage entry. Includes a full WASM adapter, meta crate, and CLI interactor. Teams add profiling contracts when they are preparing to change something — combined with the Supernova `rc/` activity, this likely informs a storage-gas schedule adjustment in an upcoming chain or VM release.

### 3. CVE-2025-64170 window widens

[#2374](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-rs/pull/2374) (dependabot, open, last updated May 22) — the rpassword 7.3.1 → 7.5.0 bump fixing CVE-2025-64170 has been open three days with no merge and no v0.66.1 tag. Impact is scoped to `sc-meta wallet new` users creating keystores interactively on the CLI. The fix is a one-line dependency bump; the delay suggests the team may be batching it with other post-release fixes rather than shipping a point release for a single dep bump.

### 4. mx-sdk-nestjs: OIDC npm provenance

[#262](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-nestjs/pull/262) + [#263](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-sdk-nestjs/pull/263) (stefangutica, merged May 22) — removes `NPM_TOKEN` from publishing secrets and switches to GitHub OIDC with `publishConfig.provenance: true` across all packages. Cryptographically signed provenance means consumers can verify that published npm packages were built from the exact tagged commit. Low-drama but a meaningful step in supply-chain hardening for the NestJS SDK ecosystem.

### 5. mx-exchange-service: coordinated modernization

[#1761](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-exchange-service/pull/1761) + [#1762](https://github.com/multiversx/mx-exchange-service/pull/1762) (claudiulataretu, merged May 21–22) — two consecutive PRs in 48 hours: Node.js 18 EOL → 24 LTS, pnpm 10, tini in Docker, multi-stage Dockerfile; then TypeScript 4.8 → 5.9, ESLint 8 → 9 flat config, Prettier 2 → 3, Jest 29 → 30, 127 packages dropped. Back-to-back modernization PRs of this scope typically precede a new service release rather than being routine maintenance — watch for an exchange-service tag within the next week.
